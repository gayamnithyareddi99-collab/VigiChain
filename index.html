ABOUT VIGICHAIN MEDSECURE

Building Intelligent Compliance
for the Future of UK MedTech

VigiChain MedSecure is a UK-focused SaaS RegTech platform engineered to modernise post-market surveillance (PMS) for medical device manufacturers operating under the strengthened MHRA 2025 regulatory framework.

The platform replaces fragmented spreadsheets and manual vigilance systems with an integrated infrastructure combining predictive AI monitoring, blockchain-backed audit trails, and automated smart-contract reporting.

Designed specifically for SMEs, VigiChain embeds compliance into daily operational workflows ‚Äî enabling real-time trend detection, structured PSUR/PMSR generation, CAPA automation, and regulator-ready documentation.
